Recipe 1: Pink Pasta

This is a delicious pasta recipe with a pink sauce.

  • Ingredients:
    • Pasta
    • Tomato sauce
    • Heavy cream
    • Butter
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook the pasta.
    2. In a pan, mix tomato sauce, heavy cream, and butter.
    3. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
    4. Pour the sauce over the cooked pasta.

Recipe 2: Pink Smoothie

A refreshing pink smoothie for a healthy start to the day.

  • Ingredients:
    • Strawberries
    • Yogurt
    • Banana
  • Instructions:
    1. Blend strawberries, yogurt, and banana until smooth.
    2. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
